Abnormal Psychology

It covers a broad range of disorders from depression to obsessive compulsive disorder to personality disorder.

  1. Psychoanalytic Approach - Many abnormal behaviors from unconscious thoughts, desires and memories.
    -believed to influence conscious actions
    -Analyzes memories, behaviors, thoughts and dreams. These can uncover and deal with feelings that lead to maladaptive behaviors and distress.

  2. Behavioral Approach - Focuses on observable behaviors
    -Behavioral therapy focuses on reinforcing positive behaviors and not reinforcing maladaptive behavior
    -Behavioral approach targets only behavior, not underlying causes

  3. Medical Approach - Focuses on biological causes of mental illness, emphasizing the understanding of the underlying cause of disorders. What is genectically inheritated, related to physical illnesses, infections and chemical imbalances.

  4. Cognitive Approach - Focuses on how internal thoughts, perceptions and reasoning can contribute to psychological disorders.
    -Treatments focus on helping individuals change thoughts/reactions
