Developmental Psychology

This sub field focuses on how humans grow physically, socially, emotionally and how their brains form.

Name Description
Prenatal Seeking to understand how earlier influences on development can impact later growth during childhood. Psychologists look at how fetuses respond to stimuli.
Early Childhood Looking at achieving physical, cognitive and emotional milestones
Adolescence Children can experience psychological turmoil They test their limits and explore their identity.
Early Adulthood Form bonds, intimacy, and close friendships
Middle Adulthood Where someone develops a sense of purpose and help contribute to society. They will most likely leave a mark on the next generation.
